Where We Serve

Hybrid in-person and virtual speech therapy services across the Columbia River Gorge region, with teletherapy available throughout Oregon, Washington, and Alaska.

Hybrid Therapy

In-Person + Virtual

Available in Columbia River Gorge communities:

  • • In-home evaluations and some therapy sessions
  • • Virtual sessions via secure platform
  • • Alternating schedule that works for your family
  • • All the benefits of both delivery methods

Teletherapy Only

Virtual Sessions

Available for out-of-range communities:

  • • All sessions conducted online
  • • Secure, HIPAA-compliant platform
  • • Interactive digital therapy tools
  • • Flexible scheduling options
