In-Home Services Include:

  • Comprehensive speech and language evaluations
  • Hybrid therapy sessions (alternating with virtual)

Why In-Home Evaluations?

  • More accurate assessment in natural environment
  • No travel stress for children and families
  • Better observation of daily communication patterns
  • Convenient for rural Gorge communities
