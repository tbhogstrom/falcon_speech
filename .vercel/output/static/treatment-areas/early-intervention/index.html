Early Intervention (Ages Birth to 5)

Supporting your child's communication development during the most important years for brain growth

Some younger children demonstrate delays in their communication skills. Early intervention helps support your child's communication skills during the most important years for brain development. For toddlers and preschoolers, therapy is play-based, engaging, and rooted in everyday routines.

We focus on:

  • Helping your child learn to understand and use words
  • Coaching caregivers to support communication during meals, playtime, and daily routines
