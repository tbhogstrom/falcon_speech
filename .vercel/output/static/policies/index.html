What You Need to Know

Payment & Insurance

We are a private-pay practice and do not bill insurance directly. This allows us to provide high-quality, personalized care without administrative limitations.

Benefits:

  • • More time focused on your child's progress
  • • Flexible, individualized services
  • • Manageable caseload for highest quality care
  • • No insurance paperwork delays

We provide superbills for possible out-of-network reimbursement.

Session Policies

Our policies are designed to ensure consistent, high-quality service while being fair to all families.

Key Points:

  • • 24-48 hour cancellation notice required
  • • Two sick-day cancellations per year without penalty
  • • Makeup sessions offered when available
  • • Weather and holiday accommodations
