Who We Are

Meet the speech-language pathologist behind Falcon Speech Services

Kailey Falcon

Licensed Speech-Language Pathologist

Kailey is a licensed speech-language pathologist serving families throughout Oregon, Washington, and Alaska. She specializes in neurodiversity-affirming therapy and takes a strengths-based approach to supporting each child's unique communication journey.

With a passion for in-home evaluations and hybrid therapy models, Kailey brings speech therapy directly to families, creating comfortable environments where children can thrive.

Our Approach

Neurodiversity Affirming

We celebrate different ways of communicating and focus on each child's unique strengths and interests.

Family-Centered

We provide therapy in your home environment and work closely with families as partners in the therapeutic process.

Flexible & Innovative

Our hybrid model combines the best of in-person and virtual therapy to meet each family's unique needs.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, neurodiversity-affirming speech therapy that celebrates each child's unique way of communicating while supporting families in the Columbia River Gorge and beyond.

What We Believe

  • Every child has a unique way of communicating that should be celebrated
  • Families are the experts on their children and equal partners in therapy
  • Therapy should focus on strengths, not deficits
  • Quality care should be accessible and convenient for families

Why Choose Us

  • Licensed in Oregon, Washington, and Alaska
  • Specialized in neurodiversity-affirming approaches
  • Innovative hybrid therapy model
  • Manageable caseload for personalized attention
